Think globally

People should have a global perspective on environmental and social issues and think about the impact of their actions on
the entire planet. At the same time, it also encourages
people to take concrete steps in their own communities to help address these issues.
This can include volunteering for local environmental organizations,
reducing energy consumption at home, and supporting sustainable business practices.
By thinking and acting locally, individuals can make a difference and contribute to solving global problems.




Nature Trusts

together we can save the planet

Nature trusts, also known as conservation trusts, are organizations that work to preserve and protect natural areas and biodiversity.

They achieve this goal by acquiring lands and waters, and managing them for conservation and sustainable use.

our services

planting

Planting trees helps to absorb carbon dioxide and reduce air pollution. It also provides habitat for wildlife and increases biodiversity. Planting also helps to prevent soil erosion and improve soil health. Trees provide shade and can help to lower urban temperatures. Communities can come together to participate in tree planting events to enhance community bonding.

recycle

Recycling reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators. It conserves natural resources such as timber, water, and minerals. Recycling also reduces greenhouse gas emissions from the manufacturing of new products. It creates jobs in the recycling and manufacturing industries. Participating in recycling programs is a simple and effective way for individuals to help protect the environment.

water saving

Water harvesting and storage helps to conserve water and reduce dependence on municipal supply. It helps to recharge groundwater levels and improve water security. Stored water can be used during times of water scarcity and drought. It also helps to reduce the amount of runoff and flooding during heavy rains. Communities can implement rainwater harvesting systems at household, neighborhood and city-level to improve water management.

tree saving

Protecting existing trees helps to preserve ecosystems and wildlife habitat. Mature trees provide numerous environmental benefits such as air purification and carbon sequestration. Preserving old growth forests helps to maintain genetic diversity and stability of forest ecosystems. Trees also have cultural and historical significance, and can be landmarks in communities.

animals saving

Protecting wildlife helps to maintain biodiversity and preserve fragile ecosystems. Endangered species need protection to avoid extinction. Preserving wildlife also helps to maintain the balance of nature and protect against the impacts of invasive species. Many wildlife species play important roles in pollination, seed dispersal, and pest control.

solar panel

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, reducing reliance on fossil fuels. They produce clean and renewable energy, reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Solar panels can provide reliable power to remote and off-grid locations. They require low maintenance and have a long lifespan. Implementing solar panels on a large scale can help to create jobs in the renewable energy sector and lead to a more sustainable future.
